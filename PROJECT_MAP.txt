================================================================================
WORD LIST SYSTEM (Answering Your Question!)
================================================================================

Q: "Will 500 words slow down the site? Will it be on a subpage?"

A: NO SLOWDOWN. NO SUBPAGE.

How it works:
  1. server/battleWords.js = Simple JavaScript array
  2. Loaded into memory when server starts (once)
  3. 500 words = ~5KB (tiny - your logo is probably 50KB)
  4. When match created, picks 2 random words (0.0001 seconds)
  5. Only 2 words sent to browsers, not all 500

Performance impact: ZERO âœ…

No subpage needed:
  - Modern apps don't use separate pages for data
  - It's just in the server's memory (like having a book on your desk)
  - Instant access anytime
